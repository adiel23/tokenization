# Long-Term Objectives

link: [Long-Term Objectives](strategy/long-term-objectives.md)

## Objectives

1. Deliver end-to-end marketplace reliability from asset submission through settlement.
2. Keep the platform secure with rate limiting, audit logs, and safer custody abstractions.
3. Expose clear health, metrics, and alerting signals for operators.
4. Maintain wiki-quality documentation for contributors, users, and administrators.
5. Support a smooth path from beta readiness to mainnet deployment.
6. Preserve Bitcoin-first design while allowing future expansion into optional integrations.
